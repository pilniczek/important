A candidate can claim to possess various soft skills on a resume. But these abilities are inherently difficult to measure on paper. So how can hiring managers suss out the hard facts when it comes to soft skills?

When evaluating a candidate's soft-skill savvy, interviewers need to rely on questions that elicit real-world descriptions illustrating how a candidate uses those abilities at work. Here are some interview questions an employer can use to evaluate today's most coveted candidate characteristics:

## Communication questions

1. Tell me about a successful presentation (to the wide audience or your manager alone) you made, and what made it so.
   &#91;[[skills-tabulka#Spolupráce|Spolupráce 2]], [[skills-tabulka#Spolupráce|Spolupráce 3]]&#93;
2. Explain how you resolved a miscommunication on your team or how you had to convince someone resistant to an idea or a change.
   &#91;[[skills-tabulka#Spolupráce|Spolupráce 10]], [[skills-tabulka#Spolupráce|Spolupráce 9]]&#93;
3. Describe a time when you had to work cross-functionally with multiple teams or departments.

## Self-management questions

1. How do you organize your day? What routines or tools do you use?
   &#91;[[skills-tabulka#Spolupráce|Spolupráce 6]], [[skills-tabulka#Spolupráce|Spolupráce 8]]&#93;
2. How do you work with distractions during the workday?
3. How do you motivate yourself to get the least appealing thing done?

## Professionalism questions

1. What do professionalism and ownership mean to you?
2. Tell me about a time when YOU TOOK ON extra responsibility, went beyond your role, or initiated an improvement of a process.
   &#91;[[skills-tabulka#Sebeřízení|Sebeřízení 4]], [[skills-tabulka#Spolupráce|Spolupráce 11]]&#93;
3. Tell me about a time when YOU WERE ASKED TO TAKE ON extra responsibility or do something you had never done before, or something outside your job description.
   &#91;[[skills-tabulka#Sebeřízení|Sebeřízení 5]]&#93;

## Lessons learned

1. What is your biggest mistake you made in past year? What did you learn?
   &#91;[[skills-tabulka#Sebeřízení|Sebeřízení 2]], [[skills-tabulka#Sebeřízení|Sebeřízení 1]]&#93;
2. What is the biggest mistake your team made in the past year? What did you learn?
   &#91;[[skills-tabulka#Sebeřízení|Sebeřízení 2]], [[skills-tabulka#Spolupráce|Spolupráce 7]]&#93;
3. Tell me about the biggest change you've had to deal with? How did you adapt to that change?
   &#91;[[skills-tabulka#Sebeřízení|Sebeřízení 5]]&#93;

## Motivation

1. What are the three things that are most important to you in a job?
2. What would make you choose our company over others?
3. How do you educate yourself? Why do you want to become better over time?
   &#91;[[skills-tabulka#Sebeřízení|Sebeřízení 4]], [[skills-tabulka#Doplňková témata|Doplňková témata 4]]&#93;

## Teamwork

1. Tell me about one of your favorite experiences working with a team. Why this experience? How did you contribute?
2. Describe the best partner or supervisor you've worked with. Why is it him/her?
   &#91;[[skills-tabulka#Spolupráce|Spolupráce 1]], [[skills-tabulka#Spolupráce|Spolupráce 11]]&#93;
3. Tell me about the last time something significant didn't go according to plan. What was your role? What was the outcome?
   &#91;[[skills-tabulka#Sebeřízení|Sebeřízení 2]], [[skills-tabulka#Spolupráce|Spolupráce 7]]&#93;
4. Tell me about a time when you led by example. What did you do and how did others react?
   &#91;[[skills-tabulka#Spolupráce|Spolupráce 11]], [[skills-tabulka#Spolupráce|Spolupráce 1]]&#93;
5. How do you share knowledge with team?
   &#91;[[skills-tabulka#Spolupráce|Spolupráce 1]], [[skills-tabulka#Spolupráce|Spolupráce 2]], [[skills-tabulka#Spolupráce|Spolupráce 3]], [[skills-tabulka#Code quality, security a testování|Code quality 3]], [[skills-tabulka#Nástroje, prostředí a workflow|Nástroje 15]]&#93;
6. When was the last time you asked for direct feedback? Why?
   &#91;[[skills-tabulka#Spolupráce|Spolupráce 4]], [[skills-tabulka#Spolupráce|Spolupráce 5]]&#93;

## The most important

1. Why do you do the work you do?
2. Why do you do your work the way you do it?
